## Digital Payment Security Protocol – UPI Fraud Prevention Framework  
**Prepared by:** Tushar Chauhan | 25BAI11335  

---

###  1. Cognitive Awareness (Human Layer Security)

- Do not react instantly to messages that create **urgency, fear, or excitement** — pause and verify.  
- Understand that most cyber frauds exploit **human psychology, not technical flaws**.  
- Always question: *“Why is this person asking me to act quickly?”*  
- Avoid decision-making under pressure, especially in financial matters.  

---

###  2. Transaction Verification Layer

- Before approving any UPI request, confirm:
  - Who initiated it?  
  - Why is money being requested?  
  - Is it a **“Pay” request disguised as receive money”?**  
- Cross-check transactions through a **secondary channel** (call or known contact).  
- Never rely solely on screenshots as proof of payment — they can be easily fabricated.  

---

###  3. Credential & Access Control

- UPI PIN, OTP, and passwords must remain **strictly confidential** — no exceptions.  
- Avoid saving sensitive information in notes, screenshots, or unsecured apps.  
- Enable **multi-layer protection**: device lock + app lock + SIM lock (if available).  

---

###  4. Device & Network Security

- Use only **secure and private networks** for financial transactions.  
- Regularly update your device to patch **security vulnerabilities**.  
- Avoid installing third-party APK files or modified apps.  
- Review app permissions and remove unnecessary access (SMS, contacts, etc.).  

---

###  5. Behavioral Red Flags (Fraud Indicators)

Be alert if you notice:

- Requests involving **“approval to receive money”**  
- Unknown individuals insisting on **immediate action**  
- Offers involving **cashback, rewards, or urgent payments**  
- Repeated collect requests within a short time frame  

These are strong indicators of attempted fraud.

---

###  6. Damage Control Strategy (If Fraud Occurs)

- Immediately contact your bank and **freeze your account if needed**  
- Report the incident within minutes via official channels  
- Do not delay — early reporting significantly increases recovery chances  
- Preserve evidence (screenshots, transaction IDs, messages)  

---

### 🇮🇳 7. Official Reporting Channels (India)

-  National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
-  Helpline: **1930**  
-  Inform your bank or UPI provider instantly  

---

###  Final Insight

Cybersecurity is not just a technical responsibility but a **behavioral discipline**.  
In UPI fraud cases, the user unknowingly authorizes the transaction — making awareness, patience, and verification the most powerful defense mechanisms.
