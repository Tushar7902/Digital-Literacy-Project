## Social Media Etiquette

### ✅ Do’s
- Be respectful and polite in your posts, comments, and messages  
- Double-check information before sharing it online  
- Keep your personal information safe by using proper privacy settings  
- Give credit to original creators when sharing their content  
- Use social media to build a positive and professional image  

### ❌ Don’ts
- Avoid posting anything offensive, abusive, or inappropriate  
- Do not share too much personal or sensitive information  
- Stay away from online arguments, trolling, or negativity  
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified information  
- Avoid using inappropriate language or images
